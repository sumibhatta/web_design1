Who We Are

A well known American photographer, Sally Mann quotes, 'Photographs open doors into the past, but they also allow a look into the future.


Obviously, a good photograph has such quality. For that photographer should have artistic eyes; brilliant mind, and tender heart working together.


Photography is an art of observation. How photographer observes and perceives the object is photography. It is like finding something valuable in an ordinary place. And, wedding photography is more than this. We have to capture each and the best moments of wedding rituals to create a wonderful wedding story. Our team believes that in a wedding ceremony every moment is important. So, we don't let go any ritual uncaptured.
